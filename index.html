Research spotlight

ZSR Train Delay Prediction

Ongoing research paper Active development

This is an ongoing ML research project, not a finished product launch. The goal is to model train delays with robust features and publish findings as a paper.

  • Two-stage LightGBM setup: classifier for delay likelihood, regressor for delay magnitude.
  • Graph-based features built with NetworkX to represent network context.
  • Continuous MongoDB scraping pipeline for fresh operational data.

Current milestone: stabilize evaluation and prepare paper-ready experimental notes.
